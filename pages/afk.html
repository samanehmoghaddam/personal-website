Away From the Keyboard (AFK)

When the models are training, I’m probably doing one of these…

Vacations

Exploring new places, food, and stories—usually with a camera in hand. I recharge best when curiosity sets the itinerary, wandering from quiet paths to busy markets and collecting tiny, memorable details along the way.

Board Games

I’m a little addicted—in the healthy, strategy-brain way. From co-ops to crunchy Euros, I love the mix of logic, table talk, and lightweight chaos with family and friends. Best kind of debugging is when it happens over cardboard.

Cycling

Two wheels, clear head. It’s my favorite way to get unstuck on a tricky idea and enjoy the ride between thoughts. Long stretches, steady cadence, and the occasional race against a sunset—perfect.

Cooking New Recipes

Creative, experimental, slightly improvisational—just like a good research sprint. I love testing new recipes and tweaking spices until the flavors click. Dinner is my favorite mini-project with instant feedback.
