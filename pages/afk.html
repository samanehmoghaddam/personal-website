Away From the Keyboard

When the models are training, I’m probably doing one of these…

Vacations

I love staying connected with nature. It feels like it recharges my batteries. I enjoy exploring new places, food, and stories, letting curiosity set the path as I wander from quiet trails to lively streets and collect the small, memorable moments along the way.

Board Games

I’m a little addicted to playing board games with friends and family. From peaceful engines like Wingspan to the pure silliness of Cash’n Guns, I love the mix of logic, table talk, and just the right amount of chaos. Honestly, the best kind of debugging happens around a table covered in cardboard.

Cycling

Two wheels, clear head. I find a lot of peace in nature—new sights, fresh air, and that steady wind from the front. Cycling helps me think through tricky ideas, get some exercise without it feeling like a workout, and spend easy, relaxed time with my family.

Cooking New Recipes

Creative, experimental, slightly improvisational, just like a good research sprint. I love testing new recipes and tweaking spices until the flavors click. Dinner is my favorite mini-project with instant feedback.
