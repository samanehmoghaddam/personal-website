I enjoy translating across technical, business, and user perspectives to ensure shared understanding and to deliver AI systems that are both rigorous and scalable.

I care deeply about AI that is not only accurate, but robust, responsible, and useful especially. My goal is to continue bridging research, engineering, and product strategy to create AI systems that deliver meaningful real-world value.
